About Me

In my academic life, I work on a number of related problems in applied mathematics. In my artistic life, I explore the intersection of randomness and human proclivity towards pattern recognition.

After graduation, I hope to consult on diverse problems in a variety of fields/industries. I will apply novel breakthroughs in risk mitigation to make economic arguments for companies to enact policies of environmental, aesthetic, and social good.
